Important public health awareness months, health observances, and key dates. Stay informed about global and national health initiatives.

Public Health Calendar

Stay informed about important health observances, awareness campaigns, and public health events throughout the year. This calendar highlights key dates when communities focus on specific health issues and prevention strategies.

2024-2025 Public Health Calendar

January

January 12 - National Youth Day - Focus on youth health and wellness - Mental health awareness for young people - Substance abuse prevention programs

January 24 - National Girl Child Day - Focus: Girl child education and health - Activities: - Awareness about gender equality - Nutrition programs for girls - Health screening camps for girls

January 30 - World Leprosy Eradication Day - Objectives: - Raise awareness about leprosy elimination - Reduce stigma and discrimination - Promote early diagnosis and treatment - Activities: Community screening camps, public education, healthcare provider training

February

February 4 - World Cancer Day - Theme: Focus on cancer prevention, early detection, and treatment - Objectives: - Reduce preventable deaths - Increase health awareness - Encourage healthy lifestyle choices - Activities: - Health camps and screening - Public awareness seminars - Educational material distribution

February 6 - Female Genital Mutilation (FGM) Zero Tolerance Day - Focus on eliminating harmful practices - Women’s health advocacy

February 9 - International Epilepsy Day - Focus: Epilepsy awareness and management - Activities: Awareness campaigns, counseling services

February 10 - National Deworming Day (India) - Focus: Parasitic disease prevention - Target: School children and vulnerable populations - Activities: Mass deworming campaigns, health education

February 11 - Women in Science Day - Recognize female scientists and healthcare professionals - Encourage women in STEM and health fields

February 20 - Social Justice Day (India) - Focus on health equity and inclusive healthcare - Community health initiatives

February 28 - National Science Day (India) - Promote scientific awareness in health - Science-based health communication

March

March 3 - World Hearing Day - Focus: Ear and hearing health - Activities: Hearing screening camps, awareness about noise pollution

March 8 - International Women’s Day - Focus on women’s health and empowerment - Maternal health, reproductive health, workplace safety - Activities: - Health camps for women - Awareness about cervical cancer, breast cancer screening - Women’s nutrition and wellness programs

March 12 - World Kidney Day - Focus: Kidney disease prevention and management - Activities: Screening for chronic kidney disease, health education

March 16 - National Vaccination Day (India) - Focus: Immunization awareness and coverage - Activities: Vaccination camps, parent education

March 20 - World Oral Health Day - Focus: Dental health and hygiene - Activities: Oral health screening, dental care education

March 21 - Down Syndrome Day - Focus: Awareness about Down Syndrome and inclusivity - Activities: Community awareness, support for families

March 22 - World Water Day - Focus: Water safety and sanitation for health - Clean water access, disease prevention

March 24 - World Tuberculosis Day - Objectives: Eliminate TB through prevention and treatment - Focus Areas: - Increase diagnosis rates - Promote adherence to treatment - Combat drug-resistant TB - Activities: - Community TB screening - Patient education - Healthcare provider training - Public awareness campaigns

March 25 - International Day for Unborn Child - Focus on prenatal health and fetal development - Maternal nutrition and care

March 26 - Epilepsy Awareness Day - Reduce stigma associated with epilepsy - Treatment and management awareness

April

April 1 - Prevention of Blindness Week - Focus: Eyesight protection and prevention of blindness - Activities: Eye screening camps, awareness about eye health

April 2 - World Autism Awareness Day - Focus: Improve quality of life for people with autism - Activities: - School and community programs - Parent support groups - Healthcare provider education

April 7 - World Health Day - Annual Theme: Varies yearly (check WHO website) - Objectives: Promote global health priorities - Activities: - Major health campaigns - Ministry of Health initiatives - Public health programs - Community engagement

April 11 - Safe Motherhood Day - Focus: Maternal health and safe delivery - Activities: Antenatal care camps, skilled birth attendance programs

April 17 - Haemophilia Day - Focus: Awareness of bleeding disorders - Activities: Support groups, treatment access advocacy

April 22 - Earth Day - Environmental health and sustainability - Impact on public health

April 25 - World Malaria Day - Focus: Malaria prevention and control - Activities: - Mass distribution of bed nets - Community education - Testing and treatment camps

April 28 - Occupational Safety Day - Focus: Workplace health and safety - Activities: Occupational health programs, safety awareness

May

May 5 - World Asthma Day - Focus: Asthma management and prevention - Activities: Screening camps, breathing exercises, inhaler technique training

May 8 - Thalassemia Day - Focus: Awareness about thalassemia and blood disorders - Activities: Support groups, transfusion services awareness

May 10 - Lupus Day - Focus: Systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) awareness - Activities: Patient education and support

May 12 - International Nurses Day - Recognize nursing profession - Focus on role in public health

May 15 - Family Day - Focus: Family health and wellness - Activities: Family health camps and counseling

May 17 - Hypertension Day - Focus: Blood pressure management and control - Activities: BP screening, dietary counseling

May 28 - Menstrual Hygiene Day - Focus: Period health and menstrual hygiene - Activities: Awareness about hygiene products, addressing stigma

May 31 - World No Tobacco Day - Theme: Emphasis on tobacco control - Objectives: - Reduce tobacco use - Address secondhand smoke - Combat tobacco industry marketing - Activities: - Community awareness programs - Smoking cessation workshops - School-based programs - Public pledges against tobacco

June

June 1 - CPR Awareness Week - Focus: Cardiopulmonary resuscitation training - Activities: CPR training camps, first aid workshops

June 4 - Day of Innocent Children Victims of Aggression - Focus: Child protection and safety - Activities: Child abuse prevention programs

June 5 - World Environment Day - Environmental health and sustainability - Impact of pollution on health

June 7 - World Food Safety Day - Focus: Safe food handling and foodborne illness prevention - Activities: Food safety awareness, hygiene programs

June 8 - Brain Tumor Day - Focus: Brain tumor awareness and support - Activities: Awareness campaigns, support groups

June 14 - World Blood Donor Day - Focus: Blood safety and availability - Activities: - Blood donation camps - Blood storage and safety education - Donor recruitment drives

June 15 - World Elder Abuse Awareness Day - Focus: Protection of elderly persons - Activities: Elderly care programs, awareness campaigns

June 18 - Autistic Pride Day - Focus: Celebration of autism and neurodiversity - Activities: Community awareness and inclusion programs

June 19 - Sickle Cell Day - Focus: Sickle cell disease awareness - Activities: Screening for hemoglobinopathies, patient support

June 21 - International Yoga Day - Focus: Physical fitness and mental wellness - Activities: - Mass yoga sessions - Community wellness programs - School-based programs

June 26 - International Day Against Drug Abuse - Focus: Substance abuse prevention - Activities: Awareness campaigns, rehabilitation services

June 27 - HIV Testing Day - Focus: HIV awareness and testing - Activities: Confidential testing camps, counseling services

July

July 1 - Doctor’s Day (India) - Focus: Celebration of medical professionals - Healthcare worker appreciation programs

July 11 - World Population Day - Family planning and reproductive health - Population dynamics and sustainability

July 28 - World Hepatitis Day - Focus: Viral hepatitis prevention - Objectives: - Eliminate hepatitis through vaccination - Improve diagnosis and treatment - Activities: - Screening camps - Vaccination drives - Health education

August

August 1 - World Breastfeeding Week - Focus: Promotion of breastfeeding - Activities: Lactation support, maternal education

August 13 - Organ Donation Day - Focus: Awareness about organ donation - Activities: Registration drives, awareness campaigns

August 20 - Mosquito Day - Focus: Vector-borne disease prevention - Activities: Awareness about dengue, malaria, Zika prevention

September

September 1 - Nutrition Week - Focus: Healthy nutrition and balanced diet - Activities: Nutritional counseling, cooking demonstrations

September 8 - Physiotherapy Day - Focus: Rehabilitation and physical health - Activities: Therapy camps, exercise programs

September 10 - World Suicide Prevention Day - Focus: Mental health awareness and suicide prevention - Objectives: - Reduce stigma around mental illness - Provide support resources - Promote help-seeking behavior - Activities: - Mental health awareness programs - Counselor training - Support group meetings - Public education campaigns

September 14 - First Aid Day - Focus: First aid training and awareness - Activities: First aid workshops, emergency response training

September 17 - World Patient Safety Day - Focus: Healthcare quality and patient safety - Activities: Safety awareness programs, incident reporting

September 21 - Alzheimer’s Day - Focus: Dementia awareness and support - Activities: Cognitive health programs, caregiver support

September 25 - Pharmacists Day - Focus: Role of pharmacists in healthcare - Healthcare professional appreciation

September 26 - World Contraception Day - Focus: Reproductive health and family planning - Activities: Contraceptive counseling, family planning services

September 28 - World Rabies Day - Focus: Rabies prevention and post-exposure prophylaxis - Activities: Awareness campaigns, vaccination programs

September 29 - World Heart Day - Focus: Cardiovascular health and disease prevention - Activities: Heart health screening, lifestyle modification programs

October

October 1 - International Day of Older Persons - Focus: Elderly health care and well-being - Activities: - Senior health camps - Fall prevention programs - Chronic disease management

October 8 - World Sight Day - Focus: Eye health and blindness prevention - Activities: Vision screening camps, eye care education

October 10 - World Mental Health Day - Theme: Mental health and well-being - Objectives: - Raise awareness about mental health - Reduce stigma - Promote help-seeking - Activities: - Awareness campaigns - Mental health counseling camps - Educational seminars - Support group meetings

October 11 - International Day of the Girl Child - Focus: Girl child health and education - Activities: Health camps for girls, empowerment programs

October 12 - World Arthritis Day - Focus: Arthritis awareness and management - Activities: Joint pain screening, exercise programs

October 13 - World Thrombosis Day - Focus: Awareness of blood clots - Activities: Thrombotic disease awareness, vascular health

October 15 - Global Handwashing Day - Focus: Hand hygiene and disease prevention - Activities: Handwashing awareness campaigns

October 16 - World Anaesthesia Day - Focus: Role of anesthesia in safe surgery - Healthcare professional recognition

October 20 - World Osteoporosis Day - Focus: Bone health and osteoporosis prevention - Activities: BMD screening, calcium/vitamin D education

October 24 - World Polio Day - Focus: Polio eradication efforts - Activities: Immunization awareness, vaccination campaigns

October 29 - World Stroke Day - Focus: Stroke awareness and prevention - Activities: - Risk factor screening - Public awareness - Healthcare provider training

November

November 7 - Cancer Awareness Day - Focus: Cancer prevention and early detection - Activities: Cancer screening camps, risk factor awareness

November 8 - World Radiography Day - Focus: Role of medical imaging in diagnosis - Healthcare professional recognition

November 12 - World Pneumonia Day - Focus: Respiratory infection prevention - Activities: Vaccination campaigns, respiratory health education

November 14 - World Diabetes Day - Theme: Focus on diabetes prevention and management - Objectives: - Increase awareness about diabetes - Promote healthy lifestyles - Improve access to treatment - Activities: - Screening camps for diabetes - Nutrition counseling - Exercise programs - Patient education sessions

November 16 - World COPD Day - Focus: Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease awareness - Activities: Respiratory screening, smoking cessation programs

November 17 - World Epilepsy Day - Epilepsy awareness and seizure management - Reduce stigma and discrimination

November 19 - World Toilet Day - Focus: Sanitation and hygiene - Activities: Sanitation awareness, water and sanitation programs

December

December 1 - World AIDS Day - Theme: HIV/AIDS awareness and combating stigma - Objectives: - Promote HIV testing - Increase awareness about prevention - Support for people living with HIV/AIDS - Activities: - Mass awareness programs - Testing and counseling camps - Support group meetings - Educational seminars

December 3 - International Day of Persons with Disabilities - Accessibility and inclusion in health services - Disability-inclusive health programs

December 12 - Universal Health Coverage (UHC) Day - Focus: Accessible and affordable healthcare for all - Activities: UHC awareness campaigns, health equity initiatives
