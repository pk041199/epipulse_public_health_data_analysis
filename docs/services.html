Our Services

EpiPulse provides accessible healthcare services through our online OPD platform. We offer general consultations and specialized care in key areas of public health concern. All consultations are conducted via secure video calls using Google Meet.

General OPD

Our general OPD provides primary healthcare consultations for common health issues, preventive care, and health education.

Services Offered:

  • Routine health check-ups
  • Common illness diagnosis and treatment
  • Health counseling and education
  • Preventive care advice
  • Referral to specialists when needed

Consultation Fee: ₹200 per session (30 minutes)

Available: Monday to Saturday, 9 AM - 6 PM

How to Book an Appointment

For Patients:

  1. Choose Service: Select the type of consultation you need from the options above
  2. Fill Booking Form: Click the “Book Appointment” button below and complete the form
  3. Provide Details: Include your name, contact information, preferred date/time, and brief description of your health concern
  4. Confirmation: You’ll receive a confirmation email/SMS with your appointment details
  5. Join Consultation: At the scheduled time, click the Google Meet link provided in your confirmation

Managing Appointments

For Our Team:

We use integrated tools to manage appointments efficiently:

1. Google Forms for Booking

  • Automatic form responses sent to team email
  • Responses stored in Google Sheets for easy access
  • Real-time notifications for new bookings

2. Google Calendar for Scheduling

  • Centralized calendar for all appointments
  • Color-coded by service type
  • Automated reminders and notifications

3. Appointment Dashboard

  • View all upcoming appointments
  • Track consultation status
  • Generate reports and analytics

Daily Workflow:

  1. Morning Check: Review Google Forms responses and update calendar
  2. Appointment Confirmation: Send Meet links to confirmed patients
  3. Consultation: Join meetings at scheduled times
  4. Documentation: Record consultation details in patient logs
  5. Follow-up: Send prescriptions and recommendations via email

Tools Setup:

Google Form Setup:

  1. Create a new Google Form with fields: Name, Phone, Email, Service Type, Preferred Date/Time, Health Concern
  2. Set up email notifications for form responses
  3. Link responses to a Google Sheet for data management

Google Calendar Setup:

  1. Create a shared calendar for appointments
  2. Set up different calendars for each specialty
  3. Enable notifications and reminders
  4. Share calendar with team members

Google Meet Integration:

  1. Use a dedicated Google account for consultations
  2. Create recurring Meet links for each time slot
  3. Embed Meet links in confirmation emails
